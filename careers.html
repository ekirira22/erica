Career Policies and Sellers Code of Conduct

All sellers are expected to adhere to the following policies when listing products on Ericaapparel. Seller offenses and prohibited content can result in suspension of your Ericaapparel account.



Seller Code of Conduct


This policy requires that sellers act fairly and honestly on Ericaapparel to ensure a safe buying and selling experience. All sellers must:

  • Provide accurate information to Ericaapparel and our customers at all times
  • Act fairly and not misuse Ericaapparel’s features or services
  • Not attempt to damage or abuse another Seller, their listings or ratings
  • Not attempt to influence customers’ ratings, feedback, and reviews
  • Not send unsolicited or inappropriate communications
  • Not contact customers except through Buyer-Seller Messaging
  • Not attempt to circumvent the Ericaapparel sales process
  • Not operate more than one selling account on Ericaapparel without a legitimate business need
